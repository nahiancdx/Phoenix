Got Questions?

Frequently Answered Questions

We believe in absolute transparency. Here are quick answers to some common questions we get from homeowners.

Are you licensed, bonded, and insured in Arizona?
Yes. We ensure all residential jobs are executed strictly to the 2023 National Electrical Code (NEC) standards. When doing jobs that require municipal code closures, we coordinate with engineers and pull all required permits so your insurance remains fully protected.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency outage?
We pride ourselves on our 24/7 responsiveness. For severe situations (smoke, burning smell in electrical boxes, total loss of power in the summer), we immediately dispatch a technician. Typically, we are on site in under two hours.
Can you install EV chargers and run cabling to detached garages?
Yes! Tracy and Brayden specialize in installing Tesla and universal EV charging equipment. We run dedicated 240V lines, handle complex concrete digging or underground trenching to detached garages, and ensure your panel has sufficient headroom to support charging loads.
What happens if my circuit breaker refuses to reset?
If a breaker trips immediately when reset, do not force it. It indicates a safety lock preventing an active short circuit or overload. Call us at (602) 780-1140 immediately. We will help you troubleshoot it safely or send Brayden out to replace the damaged panel components.
